Here is some information on how to use this site.

1. Upload multiple files.

  • Click on the "Upload Log File" button found on the home screen or the "Upload" button found in the upper right hand corner of the page.
  • Then click on the "Choose Files" button.
  • Finally, hold down the "ctrl" key while selecting the files to be opened.

2. Zoom in and out

  • Click and hold the mouse on the starting position of the start of the interval to zoom in.
  • Drag the mouse over to the end of the interval.
  • Release mouse and view the graph zoomed in.
  • Click the "Reset zoom" button to zoom out of the graph back to the first interval range of when the file was first loaded.

3. Select a specific series to view

  • In the legend of the chart, click the series that should not be included in the chart.

4. Change the interval

  • In the text box next to the word "Interval", either type in a number or use the arrow keys to indicate the desired time interval.
  • Click the "Update" button.

5. Print or download a graph

  • In the upper right hand corner of each of the graphs, select the button with three lines.
  • Then choose an option to either print or download the graphs.
